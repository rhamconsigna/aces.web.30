About ACES

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is a organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant community among computer engineering students. Our mission is to provide valuable opportunities for professional development, academic support, and social engagement. By organizing workshops, seminars, hackathons, and events that will showcase the skills of computer engineering students. ACES aims to enhance the educational experience and career prospects of its members. We strive to create an inclusive environment where students can collaborate, innovate, and excel in the ever-evolving field of computer engineering.
